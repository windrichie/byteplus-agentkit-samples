In-Person Repair Guide:
1. Booking an Appointment:
   - Call our hotline at +65-800-1234-567 to schedule a repair appointment.
   - Use our website or app to book an appointment at your preferred outlet.
   - Walk-ins are also accepted, but appointments are recommended for faster service.
2. What to Bring:
   - Bring the faulty device, including the original packaging and accessories if possible.
   - A valid receipt or digital invoice is required for all warranty claims.
   - A government-issued ID for identification purposes.
3. Repair Process:
   - Check-in: Provide your appointment details or walk-in and speak to a service representative.
   - Diagnosis: Our technicians will inspect the device and provide an initial diagnosis.
   - Estimate: You'll receive a repair estimate, including parts and labor costs.
   - Repair: Once you approve the estimate, we'll proceed with the repair.
   - Testing: Repaired devices are thoroughly tested to ensure they're working correctly.
   - Pick-up: You'll be notified when your device is ready for pick-up.
4. Estimated Repair Times:
   - Smartphone screen replacement: 1-2 hours (express service available).
   - Laptop keyboard or battery replacement: 2-4 hours.
   - Tablet screen or battery replacement: 1-3 business days.
   - Home appliance repair: 3-7 business days, depending on part availability.
5. In-Person Repair Fees:
   - Diagnostic fee: $20 (waived if you proceed with the repair).
   - Labor fee: $50-$150, depending on the complexity of the repair.
   - Parts cost: Varies by device and part required.
6. Out-of-Warranty Repairs:
   - We offer out-of-warranty repairs for all devices we sell.
   - Customers will be charged for parts and labor.
   - Out-of-warranty repairs come with a 90-day service warranty on the repaired part.
7. Data Security and Privacy:
   - We recommend backing up your data before bringing your device for repair.
   - Our technicians will only access your data if necessary for the repair.
   - We take data security and privacy seriously and follow all relevant regulations.
8. Frequently Asked Questions:
   - Can I wait for my device while it's being repaired? Yes, for minor repairs like smartphone screen replacements.
   - Do you provide loaner devices? Yes, for premium products while in repair for more than 2 business days.
   - What if my device can't be repaired? We'll provide you with options, including a replacement or a trade-in credit.
