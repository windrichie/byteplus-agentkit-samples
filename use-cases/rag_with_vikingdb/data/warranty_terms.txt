Warranty Terms and Conditions:
1. Standard Warranty: 12 months for parts and labor, covering manufacturing defects.
2. Extended Warranty (Optional): 
   - 2-year or 3-year extension available for purchase at time of transaction.
   - Covers same terms as standard warranty plus accidental damage (one-time claim).
3. Exclusions from Warranty:
   - Damage caused by liquid, physical abuse, or misuse.
   - Normal wear and tear (e.g., battery degradation after 1 year).
   - Unauthorized repairs or modifications.
   - Loss of data or software issues.
4. Proof of Purchase: A valid receipt or digital invoice is required for all warranty claims.
5. Transferability: Warranty is non-transferable and only applies to the original purchaser.
6. Service Process:
   - Customers should bring the device to any of our repair centers or call our hotline for large items.
   - Repairs typically take 3-7 business days, depending on part availability.
   - Loaner devices are available for premium products while in repair.
7. Local vs. Global Warranty: Unless stated, all warranties are local to Singapore only.
